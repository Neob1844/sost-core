**Conclusion: Concentrated distribution produces more sawtooth behavior at the same total hashrate.** The improvement comes from BOTH more total hash AND less concentration. More hash reduces absolute difficulty, while uniform distribution prevents the fast-miner/slow-miner oscillation cycle.

LIMITED ALPHA — OPERATOR-ASSISTED — NOT A PUBLIC EXCHANGE

The Position Desk is currently in limited alpha mode. All operations are operator-assisted. This is not yet a public trustless market.

Participation: Restricted to approved participants only. Access is not open to the public at this stage.

Fees: All applicable fees are visible per trade. Settlement fees, protocol fees, and any operator-assistance charges are disclosed before execution.

Scope: Positions are identified registry objects. Trades are signed and settled with full audit trail. See SOST DEX for full architecture and E2E Protocol for messaging.

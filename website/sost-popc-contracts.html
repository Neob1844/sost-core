Every PoPC contract is independently verifiable:

Model ASOST bond locked on SOST chain, gold balance verified via Ethereum RPC
Model BGold locked in immutable escrow on Ethereum, SOST reward delivered immediately
RegistryAll contract addresses, transaction hashes, and audit results are published in this registry

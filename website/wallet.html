Quick Guide

What is a SOST wallet? A cryptographic address derived from a BIP39 seed phrase (12 words). HD Wallet compatible. Send, receive, and custody SOST with full sovereignty.

How to receive SOST: Share your public address (starts with sost1...). Nobody can move your funds without your private key.

How to send SOST: Go to Send, enter the destination address and amount, sign with your key, and broadcast. Confirmed in the next mined block.

Security: NEVER share your seed phrase. Store it offline. The seed is the ONLY way to recover your wallet.

How OTC Works
1
Request
Submit a quote request specifying buy or sell, amount, asset type, and preferred contact method. Your request is recorded and routed to the OTC desk operator.
2
Quote
The operator reviews your request, checks available liquidity, and responds with a firm quote including price, fees, and settlement terms. Quotes are valid for a limited time.
3
Execute
If you accept the quote, the operator coordinates settlement. For positions, ownership is transferred via the signed settlement engine. For SOST, a direct transfer is executed.

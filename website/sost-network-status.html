PRE-MARKET

Status disclosures

SOST is in pre-market testing. The chain is live, blocks are produced near the 10-minute target, and miners are independent. None of that means SOST has reached market value, that it will be listed on any exchange, or that a market will exist. Read the Developer Note at the top of this page (or any page on the site) for the full statement.

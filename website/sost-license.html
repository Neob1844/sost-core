What Is This?

ConvergenceX is source-available technology. The source code is publicly readable, auditable, and open to contribution. Commercial deployment requires a license obtained via a refundable security deposit.

A license IS required to:

  • Integrate ConvergenceX into public-facing products

How to Obtain a License

Lock a refundable security deposit of $1,000 USD equivalent in SOST for one year (~52,560 blocks). The license is granted automatically and instantly upon deposit confirmation.

After one year, the full SOST deposit is returned — no fees, no deductions, no interest.

Step by Step

  1. Calculate the SOST amount: $1,000 ÷ current PoPC reference price
  2. Create an ESCROW_LOCK transaction for that amount (lock for 52,560 blocks)
  3. The license is generated automatically and signed by the Foundation
  4. Download your license JSON from the explorer or via RPC
  5. After 1 year: your SOST is automatically returned

Auto-Renewal

When your 1-year lock expires:

  • If you do NOT withdraw within 30 days (4,320 blocks): your license auto-renews for another year. No action needed.
  • If you withdraw your deposit during the 30-day grace period: your license expires. You get your SOST back.

This means: as long as you leave your deposit in place, your license stays active indefinitely. It's perpetual commitment without perpetual effort.

Why a Deposit?

The deposit is not a payment. It is a system-protection measure designed to:

  • Ensure operational users have a meaningful commitment to the ecosystem
  • Deter abuse and drive-by deployments
  • Keep the barrier genuinely recoverable (you get everything back)
  • Align commercial users with the protocol's long-term health

License Verification

Licenses are cryptographically signed by the Foundation and verifiable on-chain without contacting any centralized authority:

  1. Download the license JSON
  2. Verify the ECDSA signature against the Foundation's public key
  3. Verify on-chain that deposit_txid exists as an ESCROW_LOCK
  4. Verify that the current block < unlock_block (license not expired)

Command: python3 scripts/license_verify.py LICENSE.json

FAQ

Is this open source?

It is source-available. You can read, audit, and contribute to all code. Commercial deployment requires a license.

What happens after 1 year?

Your SOST deposit is automatically returned via the ESCROW_LOCK mechanism. You can renew by creating a new deposit.

What if SOST price changes?

The deposit amount is fixed at lock time. You get back the same number of SOST, regardless of price changes. The Foundation makes no guarantees about future SOST value.

Can I lose my deposit?

No. The deposit is locked in an ESCROW_LOCK on-chain. Only you can withdraw it after the lock period. The Foundation cannot access it.

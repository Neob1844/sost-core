SECTION 01

Three Pillars

ConvergenceX

Proof-of-Work that produces a mathematical certificate. Replaces the hash lottery with gradient descent convergence, memory-hard scratchpad dependency, checkpoint Merkle trees, and formal stability basin verification. The result is a verifiable proof that meaningful mathematical work occurred, producing useful entropy as a byproduct.

Metals Reserve

Automatic, constitutional accumulation of gold reserve. Every mined block allocates 25% to a Gold Vault. The Foundation converts accumulated SOST to tokenized gold exposure (XAUT/PAXG) through an automated pipeline with public attestation. The reserve is Heritage sealed — perpetual and untouched by default.

Proof of Personal Custody

Voluntary custody commitments audited by PoW-derived entropy. PoPC uses the entropy triple from ConvergenceX blocks to schedule random audits of gold custody — no oracle, no trusted third party, no governance. The audit schedule is a deterministic function of the proof-of-work itself.

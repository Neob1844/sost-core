How Positions Work
Model A — Self-CustodyBOND REQUIRED

In Model A, you keep tokenized gold (XAUT or PAXG) in your own Ethereum wallet and post a SOST bond as collateral. The protocol verifies your gold holdings through entropy-based audits. Higher risk, higher reward: you earn up to 20% annually on a 12-month lock, but face slashing if you fail an audit. Your bond is returned upon successful completion along with your earned SOST reward.

Model B — EscrowNO BOND

In Model B, you deposit tokenized gold into an immutable Ethereum escrow contract. No bond is required and no audits are needed because the contract is self-proving. Lower risk, lower reward: you earn up to 8% annually on a 12-month lock. The escrow contract has no admin key, no upgrade proxy, and no emergency withdrawal. Your gold is released only to you, only after the lock period expires.

What Is a Reward Right?TRADEABLE

When you create a position, you receive a reward right — a claim on future SOST rewards paid from the PoPC Pool. This reward right is a separate, transferable asset. You can sell it independently from the underlying gold position, allowing you to monetize the yield without giving up the principal. The buyer receives a “child position” containing only the reward stream.

How to TradeED25519 SIGNED

Positions are traded through cryptographically signed private messages. You can sell the full position (gold + reward) or split and sell just the reward right. Every trade message includes an ED25519 signature, a unique nonce, and an expiry timestamp. The settlement engine verifies ownership, checks for double-spend, and executes the transfer atomically with a complete audit trail.

Example — Angel Sells to PedroSCENARIO

Angel has a Model B position: 0.5 oz XAUT locked for 28 days with a 2,000 SOST reward. She needs liquidity now. Pedro is willing to wait. Angel creates a signed offer: “I sell position 8afed8fc for 9.7 SOST.” Pedro verifies the signature, checks the position, and accepts with his own signed message. The settlement engine verifies both signatures, confirms Angel is the real owner, transfers ownership to Pedro, and records the complete history. Pedro now owns the position and will collect the reward at maturity. Alternatively, Angel could sell only the reward right, keeping the gold-backed principal while Pedro buys the future yield at a discount.

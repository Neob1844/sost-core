# GeaSpirit Phase 44 — First Real Gravity Integration

**Date:** 2026-03-30

## Summary
Phase 44 integrated the first real gravity dataset (GA Complete Spherical Cap Bouguer Anomaly 2019) into GeaSpirit and measured its depth-aware contribution across three Australian zones.

## Key Results

| Zone | S2 Only | S2 + Magnetics | S2 + Mag + Gravity | Full Stack |
|------|---------|----------------|-------------------|-----------|
| Kalgoorlie | 0.697 | 0.734 | 0.709 | **0.739** |
| Tennant Creek | 0.728 | 0.819 | 0.806 | **0.841** |
| Mt Isa | 0.570 | 0.769 | **0.781** | 0.743 |

## Gravity Highlights
- Gravity OUTPERFORMS S2 at Mt Isa (0.720 vs 0.570) — first time geophysics beats satellite
- Gravity adds +0.119 over S2 at Mt Isa, +0.066 at Tennant Creek
- Most valuable where spectral baseline is weakest (subsurface deposits)
- Partially redundant with magnetics at magnetics-rich zones

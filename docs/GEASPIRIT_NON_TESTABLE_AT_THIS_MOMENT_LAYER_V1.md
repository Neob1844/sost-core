## Why This Layer Exists

- To be intellectually honest about the boundary between science and intuition
- To prevent scope creep of untestable claims into production
- To preserve the creative value of intuitive ideas without corrupting the scientific process
- To define what would need to change for each idea to become testable

## Non-Testable Ideas

### 1. Terrain Memory

- **Intuitive concept:** The ground "remembers" geological events
- **Why non-testable:** No sensor measures "memory" — sensors measure current physical state
- **Scientific residue:** Current rock properties (conductivity, density, magnetism) DO reflect
  geological history. This is standard geology, not "memory."
- **What would make it testable:** Nothing — it is already testable once rephrased as "rocks retain
  physical signatures of formation processes" (which is geology)
- **Status:** NON_TESTABLE_AT_THIS_MOMENT as stated. The testable version IS standard geology.

---

### 2. Conscious Subsurface Response

- **Intuitive concept:** The ground "responds" or "communicates" with intent
- **Why non-testable:** No protocol can measure geological "consciousness"
- **Scientific residue:** Subsurface properties DO modulate surface observables — but this is
  physics, not communication
- **What would make it testable:** Define specific measurable response to specific stimulus (which
  is just geophysics)
- **Status:** NON_TESTABLE_AT_THIS_MOMENT

---

### 3. Dowsing / Radiesthesia (Literal Mechanism)

- **Intuitive concept:** Rods/pendulums detect subsurface features
- **Why non-testable:** No controlled double-blind study has demonstrated above-chance performance
  for the claimed mechanism
- **Scientific residue:** Experienced dowsers are often excellent field geologists who
  unconsciously read terrain, vegetation, drainage patterns. This pattern recognition IS
  valuable — but it is geological knowledge, not the claimed mechanism.
- **What would make it testable:** A rigorous double-blind study with statistical significance.
  None exists.
- **Status:** NON_TESTABLE_AT_THIS_MOMENT
- **Note:** We respect traditional knowledge while being honest about mechanisms

---

### 4. Earth Frequency Mineral Detection

- **Intuitive concept:** Specific Earth frequencies indicate mineral presence
- **Why non-testable:** Schumann resonances exist (~7.83 Hz) but local modulation by mineral
  deposits at exploration scale has never been demonstrated
- **Scientific residue:** EM methods (MT, CSEM) DO use frequency-dependent response — but these
  are active/controlled methods, not passive "Earth frequencies"
- **What would make it testable:** A peer-reviewed study showing local EM spectral anomalies
  correlated with deposits at exploration scale
- **Status:** NON_TESTABLE_AT_THIS_MOMENT

---

### 5. Biological Sensitivity to Ore Bodies

- **Intuitive concept:** Plants/animals "sense" mineral deposits
- **Why non-testable:** While geobotany IS real (some plants indicate specific soil chemistry),
  the idea that organisms "sense" ore at depth has no identified mechanism
- **Scientific residue:** Geobotanical indicators ARE used in exploration (certain plants thrive
  on copper-rich soils). This is soil chemistry response, not sensing.
- **What would make it testable:** Already testable as geobotany (NDVI anomalies over mineralized
  ground) — which IS in our spectral pipeline
- **Status:** NON_TESTABLE_AT_THIS_MOMENT as stated. ALREADY_TESTABLE as geobotany.

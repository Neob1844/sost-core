# GeaSpirit — Global Observability Audit

**Date:** 2026-03-30

## Summary
GeaSpirit was tested at 3 randomly selected land coordinates (Zimbabwe, Cambodia, Kyrgyzstan) to audit global stack observability. This is NOT a mineral prospectivity claim — it is a coverage and capability assessment.

## Results
- 11 of 14 technology families are observable at ALL 3 zones

- Thermal inertia seasonal amplitude is measurable everywhere
- The 5 non-testable ideas remain non-testable at all locations
